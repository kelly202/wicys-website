WiCyS RIT Student Chapter

The WiCyS RIT Student Chapter is an all-inclusive affiliate of RITSEC dedicated to making RIT's Computing Security program more equitable.

The chapter holds meetings and social events for students to come together and share their passion for security while creating a supportive network.

"WiCyS is where the recruitment, retention and advancement of women in cybersecurity happens."

WiCyS @ RIT Partners

RITSEC

RIT's Computing Security club consists of 150+ active members who are dedicated to teaching security through community.

WiC @ RIT is an all-inclusive organization dedicated to increasing diversity in the computing field through community and opportunity.
